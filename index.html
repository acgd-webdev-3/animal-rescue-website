About us.

Founded in 2010, KAWS is a registered charity and organization dedicated to finding forever homes for unwanted, stray, abused and neglected animals in the Kindersley area in Saskatchewan. All animals taken into our care are vaccinated, dewormed, neutered/spayed and then placed in appropriate foster home until a permanent one is found.
